💻 Local Workflow Guide

Welcome! This dashboard acts as a visual "fill-in-the-blank" editor for your site copy, schedules, and DJ cards.

Step 1: Save & Preview

Click 💾 Save & Preview to temporarily cache your drafts. Open index.html in another browser tab to view your changes instantly!

Step 2: Export JSON

Once you are 100% satisfied with your changes, click 📥 Download JSON. This downloads your custom content.json file.

Step 3: Update Codebase

Place the downloaded content.json file directly inside your local website directory (overwriting the existing one).

Step 4: Push to Live Site

Push your updated code files to GitHub. Netlify will instantly detect the changes, build the site, and update it globally!
