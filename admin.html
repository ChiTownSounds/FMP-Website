⚡ Publishing Guide

Welcome! This dashboard acts as a visual "fill-in-the-blank" editor for your site copy, schedules, and flyers.

Option A: Publish to Live Site (Recommended)

Click 🚀 Publish to Live Site, enter the broadcaster passcode (773312), and your updates will immediately go live on the site!

Option B: Save Draft / Local Preview

Click 💾 Save & Preview to temporarily cache draft edits. Open your local index.html in another browser tab to view them locally.

Option C: Manual JSON Export

If needed, click 📥 Download JSON to export content.json manually. You can place it in your local folder, commit, and push manually.
